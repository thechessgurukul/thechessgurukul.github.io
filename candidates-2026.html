üìè The Format: A Marathon, Not a Sprint

The Candidates is famous for being the most grueling tournament in sports. Each player plays every other player twice‚Äîonce with the White pieces and once with Black. This Double Round-Robin format ensures that luck is minimized; only the most consistent player survives 14 rounds of elite chess.

The Time Control

Players have 120 minutes for the first 40 moves, followed by 60 minutes for the next 20 moves, and then 15 minutes for the rest of the game, with a 30-second increment starting from move 61. These "Marathon" controls mean games often last over 6 hours!
