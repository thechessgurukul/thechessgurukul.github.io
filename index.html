The Chess Advantage

Chess is more than just a game; it is a tool for life. At The Chess Gurukul, we use the board to develop essential cognitive skills.

Better Focus

Training the brain to block out distractions and concentrate on the task at hand.

Strategic Thinking

Learning to analyze situations, weigh options, and plan ahead.

Problem Solving

Developing the patience and logic to find the best solution under pressure.

Resilience

Building the maturity to handle wins with grace and losses with determination.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Beginner Level I Syllabus

Specially curated to build a rock-solid foundation in the game.

  • 1. Chess history in brief, the board, and pieces
  • 2. How chess pieces move and capture
  • 3. Point value of the chess pieces
  • 4. Profitable exchange
  • 5. Attack and defend
  • 6. Check and getting out of check
  • 7. Checkmate
  • 8. Special moves (Promotion, Castling, En Passant)
  • 9. Basic chess notations
  • 10. Draws

  • 13. Tournament rules and etiquette
  • 14. Chess related general knowledge
