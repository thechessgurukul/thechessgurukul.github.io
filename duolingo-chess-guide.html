Is Duolingo Chess safe for kids?
Yes, it is child-oriented with a clean, safe learning environment and no aggressive third-party ads during play.

Is it free?
The basic chess course is free. Like other Duolingo courses, you can choose to upgrade for extra features, but the core learning path costs nothing.

Will my child get bored?
Unlikely! The lessons are kept very short and use rewards (gems and streaks) to encourage consistent daily play.

Can total beginners follow it?
Absolutely. It is specifically designed for first-time learners who have never touched a chess piece before.
