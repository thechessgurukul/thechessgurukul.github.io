A Personal Vision

A Dream Beyond the Board

One coach. One vision. A lifetime of commitment to chess and the children who play it.

My name is CA Gaurav Bapat.

For years, chess has not just been a game for me. It has been a teacher, a discipline, and a way of shaping young minds. Through my journey with Chess Gurukul, I have seen something deeply powerful.

A single opportunity in chess can change a child's life.

The Dream

I carry a dream.

A dream to build a world-class chess academy in India. A place where any child, regardless of background, can walk in and learn, grow, and dream bigger.

I envision a space where everything a chess learner needs exists under one roof.

  • A spacious, international-standard tournament hall
  • A rich chess library filled with timeless knowledge
  • Residential accommodation for students coming from across the country
  • Dedicated training rooms, analysis zones, and mentoring spaces
  • A vibrant chess club that brings together players of all levels to learn, compete, and grow together
  • A culture that builds not just players — but thinkers, leaders, and resilient individuals

Not just an academy. But a home for chess excellence.

Why This Matters

India has immense chess talent.

Yet, access to structured training, infrastructure, and mentorship is still limited for many. This dream is not about creating champions alone. It is about creating access. It is about building a system where talent meets opportunity.

A Life Dedicated to This Vision

If this dream takes shape, I commit something deeply personal.

I will dedicate my entire life to building, nurturing, and growing this institution. Not as a project. Not as a business. But as a mission.

An Invitation

No great dream is built alone. I warmly invite you if you are:

  • A chess enthusiast who believes in the power of the game
  • A parent who sees learning through chess as a gift
  • A mentor, supporter, or well-wisher
  • A philanthropist who believes in empowering young minds
  • Someone who wishes to contribute to a meaningful long-term vision

Share this vision. Talk about it. Connect me with people who resonate with it. And if you are in a position to support — whether through guidance, resources, collaboration, or patronage — your contribution can help bring this dream closer to reality.

"One day, I hope we will look at this institution and say — this is where countless journeys began."

Until then, this remains a dream. But one I am committed to pursuing with everything I have.

— CA Gaurav Bapat, Founder, Chess Gurukul
